Race Details

▸ Course Map & Elevation

The course features rolling single-track dirt trails. Total elevation gain is approximately 1,200 ft for the 25K.

[Interactive Map Placeholder]
▸ Aid Stations & Cutoffs

We are a cupless race. Please bring a reusable flask. Aid stations will be stocked with water, electrolytes, gels, and salty snacks.

  • Aid 1 (Mile 4.5): Water & Gels
  • Aid 2 (Mile 9.0): Full Aid (Cutoff: 10:30 AM)
  • Aid 3 (Mile 13.1): Water Only
▸ Drop Bags & Crew

50K runners may leave one drop bag at the Start/Finish line, which is accessible at the halfway point (Mile 15.5). Crews are only allowed at the Start/Finish area.

▸ Awards & Swag

All finishers receive a custom wood medallion. Podium awards go to the top 3 Male/Female in both distances.

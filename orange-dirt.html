Race Details

▸ Course Maps & Trail Colors

All races are out and backs starting at the East Side pavilion (Red Trail Head).

  • 5K & 10K: Yellow Trail
  • Half Marathon: Yellow & Red Trails
  • Marathon: Yellow, Red, Green, and Blue Trails
▸ Packet Pickup & Camping

Packet Pickup: Friday from 5-8 PM at Stonecloud Brewing in Stillwater (917 S Husband). Race day pickup begins at 5:30 AM.

Accommodations: Primitive camping is available by the East Side Pavilion on Friday night. Bathrooms are available, but no showers. Call the lake directly to reserve a private site.

▸ Awards & Swag

All participants will receive a cool Finisher's Medal! Pre-registered runners receive a short sleeve t-shirt.

Awards to the top three finishers in Female, Non-Binary, and Male categories following each race. Must be present to pick up awards.
